Overview

“ Space travel is a dream for many people. However, it requires a huge amount of money. ”

So we use space balloon technology to bring people to the stratosphere in an economical and safest way for realizing "a future where everyone can experience the universe" .We will start manned spaceflight operations in 2021, aim to expand globally in 2023,and aim for sales over US $100 million in the space travel business by 2024.

The project consists of the following four projects.
  • 1.High altitude balloon development and manned space flight
  • 2.Practical development of robot high altitude balloon and stratospheric drone
  • 3.Balloon experiment, Aerial rocket launch, Aerial platform, Space elevator development
  • 4.AI, big data analytics tool and blockchain platform
